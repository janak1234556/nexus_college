Bachelor in Hotel Management (Hons)

A bachelor's degree in hotel management prepares students for careers in the hospitality industry by providing knowledge and skills in hotel operations, guest services, event management, and business administration.

Scope of BHM(Hons)
The scope of BHM (Bachelor of Hotel Management) involves learning about various aspects of the hospitality industry, including hotel operations, food and beverage management, event planning, front office management, and tourism. Graduates can pursue careers in hotels, resorts, restaurants, event management companies, and travel agencies. Hotel operations management Food and beverage management Event planning and management Front office management Tourism and travel management Restaurant management Marketing and sales in the hospitality industry Human resource management in hospitality Financial management in hotels and resorts Entrepreneurship in the hospitality sector.

Information about job placement after the course.
Our internship program provides college students with a guaranteed internship placement in premier hotels. Nexus College offers an internship in Malaysia, India, China, Bahrain, and Japan. The purpose of these programs is to provide students with hospitality management basics, to offer the possibility of gaining international experience, to prepare them to work in a professional environment as well as to increase the competitiveness of graduates in the labor market.
