Diploma in Professional Patisserie and Confectionery:

This diploma program focuses on the art of pastry making and confectionery. It equips students with the knowledge and skills required to create a variety of pastries, desserts, cakes, and confectionery items. Graduates can work as pastry chefs, cake decorators, or confectionery specialists in bakeries, hotels, patisseries, and dessert shops.

Scope of Patisserie
Pastry-making techniques and skills Dessert and cake decoration Baking science and ingredient functions Chocolate and sugar work Bread and dough preparation Artisanal and specialty breads Patisserie entrepreneurship Hygiene and food safety in pastry production Menu planning for patisseries Pastry arts research and development

Information about job placement after the course.
Our internship program provides college students with a guaranteed internship placement in premier IT companies. Nexus College offers an internship in Malaysia, India, China, Bahrain, and Japan The purpose of these programs is to provide students with hospitality management basics, to offer the possibility of gaining international experience, to prepare them to work in a professional environment as well as to increase the competitiveness of graduates in the labor market.
